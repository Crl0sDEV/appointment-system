2D Echo With Doppler

A 2D echo with Doppler is a non-invasive test that uses ultrasound to create detailed images of the heart and assess blood flow. It helps diagnose heart conditions, evaluate heart function, and monitor treatment. The procedure is painless, takes 30 to 60 minutes, and involves placing a transducer on the chest to capture images and flow data.
